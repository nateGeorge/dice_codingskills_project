Why does this exist?

I made this as a capstone project for the Galvanize Data Science Immersive.

I was curious which strains people said were best for different conditions. So I scraped all of Leafly.com's reviews (not an easy task - it took thousands of lines of code), and I used word2vec and neural networks to classify sentiment towards targeted words in reviews.

You can search for the cannabis strain people have talked about the most for any particular word.

Go on, try it out!
